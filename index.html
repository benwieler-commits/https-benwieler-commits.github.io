Privacy & Safety Policy — Ground & Grow

Effective date: September 19, 2025.
This policy explains how the Ground & Grow app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information. The App is a supportive self-help tool, not a replacement for professional medical care.

1. Scope

This policy covers data collected by the App and by any online services the App integrates with (for example, optional AI services). If you choose to enable cloud backups or AI features, additional third-party policies may apply.

2. What we collect

  • Local journal entries & check-ins — saved on your device by default. This includes feelings, intensity ratings, tags, and optional private notes.
  • App settings — preferences like neurodivergent mode, DID safe-mode toggles, and UI choices.
  • Optional data — if you enable encrypted cloud backup or clinician-sharing, backups or exported files may be uploaded to your chosen cloud provider or sent to your clinician with your explicit consent.
  • AI conversation text — if you use the AI Companion chat, the text you send will be transmitted to the selected AI provider. We do not store chat content on our servers unless you explicitly enable local or cloud logging.
  • No automatic telemetry — by default we do not collect analytics or telemetry from your device. Any analytics are only enabled with your opt-in and are minimised.

3. How we use data

We use data to provide app functionality: show past check-ins, power local search, enable backups, and to let the AI Companion generate compassionate reflections when you ask. We will never use your data for advertising.

4. Third parties & AI

If you enable the AI Companion, your messages are sent to the selected AI provider to generate a reply. The AI provider's privacy policy governs how they process that text. We recommend reviewing those providers' policies before enabling the feature.

5. Sharing & disclosures

We do not share your personal data with third parties except when you explicitly request it (for example, exporting a report to a clinician), or to comply with the law. In case of an emergency (imminent harm), the App displays crisis resources but does not automatically send data to emergency services.

6. Security

Local data is stored on-device. Backups (if enabled) are encrypted before upload. We use industry-standard encryption algorithms for data-in-transit and at-rest when using optional cloud features. No system is perfectly secure; please weigh privacy vs convenience when enabling cloud options.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Access — You can view all saved entries locally in the App.
  • Export — Export your data as an encrypted file for sharing with a clinician.
  • Delete — Delete your local data at any time from the App settings. If you enabled cloud backups, you must also delete backups from your cloud account.
  • Opt-out — You can opt out of AI logging and analytics in Settings.

8. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us at the address below to request deletion.

9. Crisis & safety

The App provides crisis contact information but is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. In the United States, call or text 988. In Canada, call or text 9‑8‑8. The App's crisis resources may vary by region in future updates.

10. Legal disclaimers

Ground & Grow is a support tool and not a healthcare provider. The App does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified professional for medical questions.

11. Changes to this policy

We will post the effective date above and notify users about major changes when possible. Continued use after changes constitutes consent to the updated policy.
